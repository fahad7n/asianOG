DR. REENA ANNIE CHACKO

Dentistry

BDS, MDS - Pediatric Dentistry (QCHP License No: D8881)

Dr. Reena Annie Chacko has pursued her BDS AND MDS from Baba Farid University of Health Sciences. She is a specialist Pediatric Dentist with over five Years of experience in providing full spectrum dental services for babies, children and adolescents. She is also trained to treat special needs children’s, un cooperative and anxious young patients. She believes building an ongoing relationship between her and her patients. She ensures that all aspects of evaluations and treatments are delivered in a family centered manner where parents take an active part every step of the way. She is proficient in English, Hindi, Malayalam and Punjabi.
